We partnered with Surround Definition to provide this demo with SHD technology, the standard for playback of 360° videos. User directed movement gives directors and producers new possibilities to communicate with their audience. Upcoming technologies like the Oculus Rift or Google Glass will make SHD content even more compelling. The SHD integration into JW Player brings 360° video into a rich and reliable playback platform. Being Flash-only now, next steps are to release the SHD engine to HTML5 and mobile. For more information or questions, please visit surrounddefinition.com.
